FLOW:
Tournament → Matches → Innings → Overs → Balls

FINAL SCHEMA FOR CREATING DATABASE
            User(id, name, role  -- organizer / player)

            Tournament(id, name, season, location )

            Team(id, tournament_id, name)

            Player(id , team_id, name, role  -- batsman/bowler/allrounder )

            Match(id, tournament_id,team1_id, team2_id,match_date, venue,overs_limit, status
            )

            Innings(
            id, match_id,
            batting_team_id, bowling_team_id,
            innings_number
            )
            Ball(
            id,
            innings_id,
            over_number,
            ball_number,   -- 1 to 6
            striker_id,
            non_striker_id,
            bowler_id,
            runs_off_bat,
            extras,
            wicket_type,
            dismissed_player_id
            )


RULES:
Ball number ∈ [1,6]

Over increments only after 6 legal balls

Wickets ≤ 10

Innings ends correctly
